FLYER REVIEW AND PAYMENT POLICY

External organizations uploading a flyer to the Enrichment Programs Section recognize their flyer is subject to review and approval by the school(s)/district(s) where they seek to post and distribute their flyer. In order for the school district to determine whether the organization's flyer meets school district requirements for flyer approval, the external organization's account information may be viewed by school district staff during the flyer approval process and as part of reports provided to school districts. The decision to approve or deny a flyer is solely determined by the controlling school(s)/district(s). Payment of Peachjar's flyer posting service fee is non-refundable once the transaction is completed. Should an external organization's flyer be denied, the posting organization will be notified electronically and a refund will be provided as an account credit after 24 hours. This delay enables the controlling school/district to undo their action and subsequently approve a flyer without the need for the external organization to repeat the uploading and checkout process. If the controlling school/district requests that a change be made to the submitted flyer, the external organization may click the “Return Credits” button on the “My Recent Flyers” page to immediately process an account credit.

Peachjar does not verify the authenticity of information provided by posting organizations that is available for review by school district staff during the flyer approval process.
